Courses Offered

Navodaya and Sainik entrance courses

Our programs are designed for students who want admission into Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Sainik Schools, Rashtriya Military Schools, and RIMC. Each course combines concept teaching, regular tests, OMR practice, and doubt clearing.

JNV

Navodaya Entrance Coaching

Complete preparation for the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test, with special focus on mental ability, arithmetic, and language skills.

  • Chapter-wise practice for JNVST pattern
  • Reasoning, maths, Telugu/Hindi/English language support
  • Weekly mock tests and previous paper practice
Classes: 5th to 6th entry
AIS

Sainik School Coaching

AISSEE preparation for students aiming to join Sainik Schools, with classroom training for maths, intelligence, English, general knowledge, and interview confidence.

  • Separate preparation for Class 6 and Class 9 entrance
  • OMR-based tests to improve speed and accuracy
  • Discipline, time management, and interview guidance
Classes: 6th and 9th entry
RMS

Rashtriya Military School

Complete RMS entrance coaching with subject lessons, previous paper practice, and weekly mock exams.

Classes: 6th and 9th entry
RIMC

RIMC Entrance Coaching

Focused preparation for written test, interview readiness, English, maths, and general knowledge.

Foundation and entrance batch
FDN
